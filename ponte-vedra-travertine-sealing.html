Stone care

Travertine deserves its own page for a reason.

It is usually a higher-value surface and the homeowner cares more about the finish. Keep the copy calm, premium, and process-driven.

  1. Inspect: look for etching, staining, moisture, and loose areas.
  2. Clean: use surface-appropriate cleaning instead of blasting aggressively.
  3. Dry: confirm conditions before sealer is applied.
  4. Seal: protect the stone while preserving a natural-looking finish.
