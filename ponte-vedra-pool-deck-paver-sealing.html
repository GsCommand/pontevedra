Pool deck care

Pool areas need cleaning and sealing done with restraint.

The copy should avoid promising miracle traction or stain removal. Sell the process: clean, prepare, sand, dry, and seal based on conditions.

  1. Organic treatment: address algae and mildew buildup.
  2. Surface prep: clean around cage edges, drains, and shaded areas.
  3. Sand: replace weak joints where water has washed sand out.
  4. Seal: apply after moisture conditions are right.
